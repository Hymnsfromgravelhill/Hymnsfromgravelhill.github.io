<p><b>Preface</b></p>
<p>The first edition of the Pacific Gospel Hymn Book was published in 1913 by Mr. James Rae to supply a felt need at the time. The circulation increased beyond expectation and there was a demand for an enlarged edition.</p>
<p>In 1946, Mr. Hector Alves responded to the demand and published The Gospel Hymn Book in a new and enlarged format. He endeavored to select only scriptural hymns, those best adapted for use in gospel meetings, also including a number of hymns suitable for believers' meetings.</p>
<p>With the increase in gospel activity in many parts, and the constant need for hymn books, various brethren and assemblies suggested additional hymns to be included. The present edition has been prepared with the prayerful desire that present needs will be met by this enlarged edition of The Gospel Hymn Book.</p>
<p>The tunes for these hymns will be found in the Music Editions of the larger hymn books, which have been noted at the top of each hymn.<br><br>Key to abbreviations:<br>R.
